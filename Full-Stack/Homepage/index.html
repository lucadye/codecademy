Our Mission

Aperture Science's mission is to advance scientific discovery through developing innovative technologies to solve complex global challenges while maintaining high ethical standards. Their team of scientists and engineers strives for excellence and creativity in their work, with the belief that knowledge and progress are essential for society's betterment.

Who We Are

Cave Johnson

Cave Johnson, Aperture Science's founder and CEO, is a risk-taking and visionary leader who nurtures talent to achieve groundbreaking scientific advancements. He invests in his employees to create a hub of scientific creativity that attracts top minds, remaining humble and dedicated to science and excellence, inspiring the next generation.

Caroline

Caroline is an accomplished employee at Aperture Science, working as the personal assistant to the CEO, Cave Johnson. She excels in both administrative work and science, contributing significantly to the development of groundbreaking technologies such as GLaDOS, which manages the company's research facilities.

Robots

Aperture Science's robots excel in any environment and are programmed to be friendly and helpful, ensuring an enjoyable experience in their testing facilities. Their design embodies Aperture Science's commitment to innovation and safety with cutting-edge technology and materials, ensuring complete safety during human interaction.
