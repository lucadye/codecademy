The Knights of the Square Table

Play, Learn, and Connect

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, the Knights of the Square Table offers a fun and supportive environment to play and learn the game of chess. We organize regular chess tournaments, games, and social events to foster a sense of camaraderie among our members and to provide opportunities to hone your skills.

Push your limits and sharpen your skills

The Knights of the Square Table is a local chess club that brings together chess enthusiasts of all levels and ages to enjoy and improve their skills in the game of chess. Our members are passionate about the strategy and intellectual challenges that chess provides, and we strive to create a welcoming and inclusive community for all members.

Join us and find chess camaraderie

Joining the Knights of the Square Table means being part of a group that values creativity, strategic thinking, and the joy of the game. So, whether you're looking to improve your chess game, make new friends, or simply have fun, we welcome you to come and play with us!
